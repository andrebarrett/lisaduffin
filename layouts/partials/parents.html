Let's face it parenting is hard

The sheer responsibility that comes with raising a human is huge and the magnitude can sometimes feel crippling.

✔︎ You’ve avidly read every parenting book you can

✔︎ You know your child’s developmental milestones like you know the back of your hand

✔︎ You have tried every parenting strategy you know of…. repeatedly

And yet you can’t help but think you are falling short; that you are missing something. Somewhere deep inside is a niggling feeling that tells you you could be doing more to empower your child

You are not alone. According to the research ...

46% of mums and 45% of dads say they feel judged all the time or nearly all the time

Almost half (48%) of all parents don’t feel they are getting the support they need when they feel stressed

69% of parents say that if they knew more positive parenting strategies they would use them
